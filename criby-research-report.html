Operating a real estate marketplace across 100+ countries is a regulatory nightmare. No competitor has attempted this scope — and for good reason.

  • Licensing: 41 US states require firm registration for brokerage; classification as marketplace vs brokerage is jurisdiction-specific
  • Data Privacy: GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, PIPL — each requires different consent, storage, and transfer mechanisms
  • Data Localization: China, Russia, India require in-country data storage — dedicated infrastructure per country
  • Financial: Money transmitter licensing if handling payments; AML/KYC obligations; OFAC sanctions screening
  • Tax: 110+ countries impose digital services VAT/GST; DAC7 reporting in EU; FIRPTA withholding on US property

Bull Case vs. Bear Case

BULL CASE

"The Booking.com Moment for Housing"

Remote work creates permanent demand. AI + verification eliminates trust barriers. First mover in nomad corridors builds unassailable density. Fintech creates 40%+ margins. Outcome: $500M-2B revenue, $5-15B valuation in 7-10 years.

BEAR CASE

"Real Estate Is Fundamentally Local"

Housing decisions require local knowledge that can't be digitized. Regulation makes global ops prohibitive. Incumbents add cross-border features faster. Result: narrows to 3-5 cities, acqui-hired or shut down within 5 years.

Historical Pattern

Every attempt at a global real estate marketplace has either narrowed, pivoted, or failed. Zero exceptions. Spotahome (85% from 8 cities), Nestpick (shut down, acquired), HousingAnywhere (pivoted B2B), Roost (shut down), Uniplaces (stagnant). The default outcome without extraordinary execution is failure.

Trust vs. Translation

Criby leads with translation (70+ languages). This is a mistake. Translation is commoditizing — Google Translate and DeepL are free and improving fast. Within 2-3 years, language barriers in online content will be functionally eliminated. Trust is the durable moat: verified landlords, property inspection, escrow, refund guarantees, review portability. Each verified transaction builds reputation data that compounds over time.
